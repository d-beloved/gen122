About PILLARS OF WEALTH

Pillars of Wealth Consulting Ltd is a consultancy outfit incorporated in Nigeria under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 1990 to provide Agriculture and Agro-allied consultancy and training services. We specialize in conducting market survey, feasibility studies, and preparation of workable and bankable pre-feasibility reports, techno-economic feasibility reports and business plan for various agricultural and agro-allied enterprises for the purpose of evaluating their technical feasibility, financial viability and economic desirability.
